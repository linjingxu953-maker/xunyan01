### 2. 状态机闭环

#### MascotStateMachine 状态转换规则

```
Idle ──────────► Listening (用户发起任务)
Listening ─────► Understanding (接收任务后)
Understanding ─► Working (简单任务直接执行)
Understanding ─► ReadingContext (需要上下文)
ReadingContext ─► Planning (规划步骤)
Planning ──────► Working (执行任务)
Planning ──────► WaitingApproval (需要确认)
WaitingApproval► Working (用户批准)
WaitingApproval► Idle (用户拒绝)
Working ───────► Reporting (生成汇报)
Working ───────► MemoryConfirm (记忆确认)
Working ───────► Completed (直接完成)
Working ───────► Error (执行失败)
Reporting ─────► Completed
MemoryConfirm ─► Completed
Completed ─────► Idle
Error ─────────► Idle
```

#### 任务取消支持

- TaskRouter 支持 CancellationToken
- CancelTask(taskId) 取消指定任务
- CancelAllTasks() 取消所有任务
